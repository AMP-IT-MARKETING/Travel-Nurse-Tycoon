# TRAVEL NURSE TYCOON — PRODUCT SPEC + GDD (v2.0: Nurse‑First)

Owner: Faraj Darrat (AMP)  •  Audience: Eng, Design, PM  •  Platform: Web (mobile‑first PWA)

---

## 0) ONE‑PAGE VISION

**Pitch:** Be the nurse. Plan your life 13 weeks at a time. Choose assignments, manage housing, licensure, and budget, work shifts, and build the career (and freedom) you want. Agencies and hospitals are partners and settings—not the player.

**Player Fantasy:** “I craft the perfect travel career: great pay, right unit, new city, rested and ready.”

**North‑star:** Respect the profession. Keep it playful, real, and never punitive.

**Soft‑launch KPIs:** D1 ≥ 30%, D7 ≥ 7%, D28 ≥ 1.5%; tutorial completion ≥ 85%; median session 2–5 min; ≥3 sessions/day for top cohort.

---

## 1) GAME PILLARS

1. **Nurse‑centric:** Career, well‑being, and choices at the center. No clinical simulation of care delivery.
2. **Authentic but light:** Real concepts (NLC licensure, agencies, stipends, housing, call‑offs) with toy‑level numbers.
3. **Mobile clarity:** Thumb‑reach UI, 1–2 taps to core actions, readable at 360–414px.
4. **Fair surprises:** Real‑life events (cancellations, float, weather, housing issues) at low frequency with recoveries.
5. **Progress you feel:** Every session advances something—docs, licenses, savings, reputation, or skills.

---

## 2) CORE & META LOOPS

**Core Loop (60–120s):** Explore assignments → Compare pay package & lifestyle → Apply via an agency → Complete credential/licensure tasks → Start → Work shifts → Earn → Rest & plan next.

**Meta Loop (days–weeks):** Build multistate licensure (NLC), add certs, collect EHR experiences, grow reputation with agencies/facilities, unlock dream cities and premium housing.

**Session Flow:**

* **Enter:** “Today” card with next shift, energy, open offers, pending docs.
* **Act:** Choose today’s focus (shift, documents, housing search, license step). Queue long tasks (license, background).
* **Reward:** Weekly payday split into taxed pay + non‑taxed stipends (game‑simplified), milestone badges, new offers.

---

## 3) SYSTEMS (NURSE‑FIRST)

### A. Assignments & Agencies

* **Offers:** Generated by agencies (AMP featured alongside fictional peers). Each offer = unit, city, start, shift pattern, pay package (taxed base + stipends), housing difficulty, EHR.
* **Acceptance Fit:** Specialty match, licensure readiness, EHR familiarity, rest balance, and reputation.
* **Renewal / Switch:** Renew same facility (lower friction) or hop—affects reputation with agencies and facilities.

### B. Licensure (NLC) & Certifications

* **NLC:** If player holds compact multistate license, more states unlock instantly; non‑compact requires a queued license task.
* **Certs:** BLS/ACLS/NRP/AWHONN etc. as timers with study boosts; unlocks units and improves offers.

### C. Credentialing & Docs

* **Kanban:** To‑Collect → In‑Review → Approved. Time gates (Fast/Standard/Slow). Upgrades reduce timers/errors.
* **Failure Recovery:** Missing doc rolls back one step, never to zero; friendly tips.

### D. Housing & Cost of Living

* **Options:** “Frugal / Standard / Premium” by city; impacts morale, commute time, and stipend utilization.
* **Search Mini‑Task:** Quick pick or deeper browse; rare “Gem Find” (cheap, high morale).

### E. Energy, Fatigue & Well‑Being

* **Energy Bar:** Shifts consume, rest restores; fatigue lowers shift quality and acceptance chance; days off boost morale.
* **Healthy Routines:** Tiny buffs from sleep, meals, exercise; toggle for “auto‑self‑care.”

### F. Finance (Game‑Simplified)

* **Weekly Payday:** (Taxed hours × base rate) + (stipends for housing + M&IE) − simple expenses (transport, housing overage).
* **Budget Goals:** Emergency fund, license fund, certification fund; visible progress rings.

### G. Reputation & Network

* **Agency Rep:** Good communication, few last‑minute changes, successful starts → better, faster offers.
* **Facility Rep:** On‑time starts and good reliability → renewal bonuses and easier schedules.

### H. Events Deck (Surprises)

Positive: Bonus shift offer, referral bonus, housing deal, license processed early, mentor assigned.
Negative (low freq): Contract cancellation, weather delay, float to similar unit, roommate issue, EHR crash orientation.
Every negative has **soft recovery** (stipend credit, rush help, backup offer).

## 6) ECONOMY & BALANCE (INITIAL)

**Tunable Bands (placeholders):**

* Base rates by unit; stipend bands by city; housing difficulty index (1–5); license timer bands (compact vs non‑compact).
* Negative event base chance per real‑day: **2–4%** with pity timers; positive events **6–9%**. **Harshness:** rare & recoverable by design.

**Formulas (pseudo):**

* **Offer Acceptance** = f(license ready, match %, energy, housing friction, reputation).
* **Credential Time** = base × (1 − upgrades − EHR familiarity).
* **Weekly Net (Simplified)** = TaxedPay + Stipends − Expenses − HousingOverage + Bonuses.

**Light Tax Sliders (client‑side only):**

* **Federal bracket slider** (preview): 10–37% bands; default auto‑calc from a simple bracket table; toggle to manual.
* **State tax slider:** 0–10% default range with presets (e.g., 0%, 3%, 5%, 7%, 9%).
* **FICA toggle:** default 7.65% employee (SS 6.2% up to wage base; Medicare 1.45%); optional “self‑employed mode” 15.3%.
* **Disclaimer UI:** “Educational, simplified—not tax advice.”

**Safeguards:** No hard fails; floor on take‑home after bad week; recovery missions.

## 10A) FTUE (FIRST‑TIME USER EXPERIENCE) — SCRIPT & WIREFLOWS

**Goal:** Get a first‑time player from zero → first shift preview in **< 90 seconds**, while teaching core verbs: Review Offer → Accept → Queue Docs → Rest/Shift.

**Intro (6–8s, skippable):** 3 comic‑panel frames with subtle parallax + captions: 1) “New city, new unit.” 2) “Choose the right contract.” 3) “Thirteen weeks, let’s roll.”
CTA: **Start my travel journey**.

**Step 1 — Avatar Lite (10–15s):** Choose **name** and **scrubs color** (optional randomize). Show avatar bust next to HUD (mood face).

**Step 2 — Pick a Vibe (8s):** Choose a starting perk (Adventure +1 energy recovery / Earnings +5% base / Balance −10% fatigue). These map to SDT needs of autonomy & competence.

**Step 3 — First Offer (20–25s):** Overlay highlights **Opportunities** tab (pulse). Open pre‑curated offer (unit = player specialty).

* Guided callout: “This is your **pay package** (taxed base + stipends). Tap to accept.”
* Secondary: “Blockers” chip shows 1 mini doc + housing choice.

**Step 4 — Mini Credential (10–15s):** Open **Credential Board** with **one** task (e.g., flu shot upload). Tap **Auto‑complete demo** (simulated 3s timer) to teach the board.

**Step 5 — Housing Pick (10s):** Three‑card pick (Frugal/Standard/Premium). Instant morale preview.

**Step 6 — First Week Preview (10s):** Back to **Home (Today)** with **Energy** bar and **Next Shift**. CTA: **Start first shift** (plays 5‑sec “shift complete” animation, grants XP + payday preview).
End with **Level 2** unlock + **AMP recruiter DM** sample message.

**Skips/Recovery:** All steps skippable; “Guide Me” button restores overlay. “Reduced Surprises” toggle offered.

**Copy blocks & tooltips:** Provided in §10B.

**KPIs to track:** `ftue_step`, `offer_accept`, `task_complete`, `week_paid_preview`.
